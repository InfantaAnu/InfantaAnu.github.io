EGG SANDWICH

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Egg 2
Bread 4
Onion 1
Tomato 1
Butter As required
Seasonings As required

Step 1: Chop the onions and tomatoes finely
Step 2: Make a rectangular hole in the center of the bread slices
Step 3: Mix 2 eggs and the chopped veggies in a bowl. Add seasonings
Step 4 : Heat a tawa and add butter
Step 5: Place the dented bread and fill the dent with the egg mixture
Step 6: Place the cut-out piece on top of the egg mixture
Step 7: Cook on both sides and serve hot
